Contact Detail
Per-Contact Equity Visualization
Inside a contact's detail view, show their individual equity score with a segmented ring. Each segment represents an interaction type. The ring IS the relationship history, compressed into one shape.

Segmented Equity Ring

Ring segments colored by interaction type. Thicker segments = more of that type. The ring tells the story of how you communicate with this person.
Calls (5)
Emails (3)
Texts (2)
Meetings (2)
Each interaction type has its own segment and color. The ring communicates channel diversity — a ring with only one color means you're over-indexed on one channel.
