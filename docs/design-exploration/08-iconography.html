Style
Two Directions
Icons need to match the editorial serif + bold orb aesthetic. Too thin feels SaaS. Too heavy competes with the orbs. The sweet spot is medium-weight rounded line icons.

A. Rounded Line (1.5px stroke)

Clean line icons with rounded caps and joins. Medium weight — not hairline, not chunky. Matches Plus Jakarta Sans's geometric personality. Think Lucide or Phosphor.

Log Interaction Picker

Type Selector Pills

Navigation
Mobile Tab Bar Icons
The bottom tab bar on mobile needs icons. Two tabs: Pulse (dashboard) and Map (orb canvas). Active tab uses brand green filled icon, inactive uses light stroke.
Pulse

Pulse

Pulse icon: Heart shape — the network's heartbeat, health pulse.
Map icon: Crosshair/target with radiating lines — spatial navigation, like a compass for your network.
Active: Solid fill in brand green.
Inactive: 1.5px stroke outline in light gray.
