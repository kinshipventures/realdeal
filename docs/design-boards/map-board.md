## What This Page Needs To Do

Give the user a spatial way to understand and enter their relationship world.

- it is the most distinct page in the product
- the orbital metaphor gives Real Deal character
- pods and categories already feel different from the list and dashboard views

## What The Page Is Missing

- stronger alignment with the newer calm shell direction
- better continuity with dashboard and detail pages
- less risk of feeling like a separate older product

- hub node
- pod orbs

## What To Keep

- the sense that this is the most brand-forward page

## What To Change

### Chrome

- bring the map controls closer to the new shell
- make the page feel less detached from the rest of the app

### Tone

- keep the specialness
- reduce any leftover "old visual system" feeling
- make it feel calmer and more premium

This page should feel like the expressive side of the same product.

Not a separate mode with a different taste level.

## Redesign Rules

- keep it special
- align the chrome with the shared shell
- let the map stay more visual than other pages
- do not flatten it into a normal dashboard page
