## What shipped last week

RealDeal hit **Alpha 0.1** - the first version with real infrastructure and a full feature set. Here's what's new:

- **New database backend** - moved everything off Airtable onto a faster, more reliable system. All existing data preserved. No more slowdowns or limits.
- **Network map redesigned** - each pod now shows a health ring so you can see relationship status at a glance. Hover any pod to see the last interaction. You can drill into a pod and search for anyone by name.
- **Equity scoring live** - every contact gets a 0-100 health score based on how recently and how meaningfully you've been in touch. There's a "How It Works" page in the app that explains the whole system.
- **Follow-up system** - pin follow-ups to contacts, and overdue items automatically surface on your dashboard. Today's Focus tells you exactly who to reach out to each day.
- **Pod sharing** - generate a shareable link for any pod. Choose which contacts to include or exclude. Recipients see a clean read-only view, no login needed.
- **Workspaces** - foundation for team access with invite system and workspace switching
- **Reports page** - see how your pods are distributed, pipeline velocity, and engagement activity over time
- **Companies page, pipeline improvements, search (Cmd+K), CSV import wizard**
- **12 bug fixes** across dark mode, navigation, mobile, and general reliability
- **"What's New" page** added to the sidebar so you can always see what's changed

Full changelog is live in the app -- click "What's New" in the sidebar to check it out.
