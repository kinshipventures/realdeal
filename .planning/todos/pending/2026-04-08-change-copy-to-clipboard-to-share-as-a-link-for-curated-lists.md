## Problem

The "Copy to clipboard" option under Export should be "Share as a link". Important for users to share curated lists, not just pods. Brielle feedback on Notion item 3.

## Solution

Replace "Copy to clipboard" with "Share as a link" that generates a shareable URL for the current filtered/curated view.
